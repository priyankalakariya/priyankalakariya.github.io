About

Where the math meets the hardware.

I'm an MS Robotics student at Northeastern University, concentrating in Electrical & Computer Engineering. Before that, I spent two years as a manufacturing engineer at Innomotics (formerly Siemens Large Drive), scheduling production for medium-voltage motors and learning, the hard way, what happens when a clean design meets a messy factory floor.

That background shapes how I approach robotics: I care less about a method being elegant on paper and more about whether it survives contact with real sensors, real noise, and real latency. Lately that's meant SLAM in GPS-denied environments, pedestrian trajectory prediction, and closed-loop navigation in dynamic scenes.

Outside of robotics, I was Technical Core for Team Anonymous at RoboCon India (All-India Rank 16), and I've led as President of a Toastmasters chapter and Chairman of my university's Roboclub.

Vehicle State Estimation

VectorNav VN-100EKFComplementary FilterGPS / UTMSensor Fusion

Heading and velocity estimation on real Boston driving data (EECE 5554, Northeastern). Corrected hard- and soft-iron magnetometer distortion from a circle-drive calibration, then fused the drift-free magnetometer heading with integrated gyro rate through both a complementary filter (α = 0.98) and an extended Kalman filter (EKF), and estimated forward velocity from high-pass-filtered accelerometer readings against a GPS/UTM baseline — characterizing how quickly an unaided IMU diverges.

EKF + comp. filterVN-100 IMUGPS-referenced

Real-Time Pedestrian Trajectory Prediction

Dual-stream network combining Social-LSTM with a Transformer pose encoder, using RTMPose + SMPL for 3D pose at 30 FPS so the model anticipates pedestrian intent before position alone would reveal it.

3D Scene Reconstruction via Structure-from-Motion

SIFTRANSACBundle Adjustment

SfM pipeline over a 62-image dataset — SIFT + GrabCut for features, Lowe's ratio test and RANSAC to reject outliers, then sparse bundle adjustment with a COO Jacobian to refine the reconstruction.
